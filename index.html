About Tomato Times

TomatoTimes was started by my daughter and I. The name originated from my daughter's cartoon strip entitled "Tomato Times" that was imagined to be from a fictional newspaper. She made the cartoon in the Brevard Art Museum summer camp near the chocolate shop. She was seven when she drew the cartoon. I (Dad) liked the name and had some TomatoTimes T-shirts and pens made. The T-shirts say "Tomato Times, Staff". We write the software together.
